About Me

My name is Xyrus. I am an 11-year-old student studying at Wellington Seventh-Day Adventist school. I love to do maths, programming and playing computer games with my friends at home. I was born in Singapore on 9th December 2010. I want to be a programmer because I like to solve problems and improve any things I can. During the school holidays, I take online courses for programming and my dad helps me if I have any questions or problems. I am working on a wiki website for my favourite game, Farming Simulator 2022. I love this game because I get to learn how to farm, drive farm vehicles, experience how much I would get paid as a farmer and find out the best things to use such as crops, vehicles and the equipment for them. The most appealing thing to me in the game is the easter eggs, which are hiding everywhere and give you a lot of money. In this website, I want to show readers the usage of different things in the game, like vehicles, how-to videos and modifications you can download to improve your game.

So enjoy my content and wait until I add more! If there are any problems, feel free to contact me at xyrusbabao@gmail.com.
