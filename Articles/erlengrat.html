Farming Simulator 22 Erlengrat Map

Written By Xyrus Babao July 22, 2022
News

The team at Giants software have just released the final ingame map - Erlengrat. The map mostly contains grass fields which are perfect for animal husbandries. It features a lakeside town with the alpine mountains in the horizon. You start with three tractors, a plow, water trailer, milk trailer and a push mower.

You can learn more at the original Farming Simulator Website.

Summary:

  • Bug fixes.

  • Added the new map - Erlengrat.
