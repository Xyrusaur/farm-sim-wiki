TT8022 Drive-Less

Written By Xyrus Babao July 20, 2022
Mods / Attachments / Sugarcane Technology

The TT8022 Drive-Less is a chopped sugarcane planter with much more capacity then the basegame planters. It also includes a solid fertiliser tank to improve your crops. The planter is slightly narrower than the basegame two row planter, but has a big capacity to keep planting sugarcane. This mod is useful if you have large fields you need to plant sugarcane and add fertiliser at the same time.

You can learn more at the original Modhub website.

The specs:

Price: $61,500

Width: 1.6m

Required horsepower: 200hp

Seed capacity: 11,000L

Solid fertiliser capacity: 1,100L

Maximum working speed: 12kph

Pros:

  • Have more fun with your potato game
  • Built-in fertiliser tank to save time.

Cons:

  • Harder to maneuver as it is a trailer
  • Expensive, not ideal for beginner farmers.
