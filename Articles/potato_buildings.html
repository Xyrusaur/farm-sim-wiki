Seedpotato Farm Buildings Pack

Written By Xyrus Babao July 21, 2022
Mods / Buildings / Productions

The Seedpotato Farm Buildings Pack adds a lot to your FS22 potato game. It includes a potato sorting shed which sorts all of your normal potatoes into three different categories - Seedpotatoes, premium potatoes and waste potatoes. Premium potatoes are almost twice as worth as normal potatoes, but even more when bagged with a potato bagger or do it altogether with the large potato sorter, as shown above. Seedpotatoes are worth slightly more when bagged, but loose seedpotatoes cost the same as normal ones. When paired with a seperate mod, Seedpotato Farm Vehicles Pack, you can fill seedpotatoes with the mod's potato planters. Last but not least, waste potatoes are worth less than normal ones, But when bagged, They will instantly be turned into pig food instead of buying some for your pigs in the store.

You can learn more at the original Modhub website.
