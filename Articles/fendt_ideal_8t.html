Fendt Ideal 8T

Written By Xyrus Babao August 28, 2022
Basegame / Vehicles / Combines

The Fendt Ideal 8T is a large and expensive combine for your fields. It comes with a decent capacity and large header. I recommend using this combine for large contracts.

You can learn more at the original Farming Simulator website.

The specs:

Price: $463,000

Max speed: 40kph

Max Working speed: 10kph

Horsepower: 538hp

Capacity: 12500 - 17100L

Width: 9 - 12m

Pros:

  • A large combine for large fields.
  • Great for farmers who have earned a decent amount of money and have large fields.

Cons:

  • Expensive, not ideal (get it?) for farmers that have little budget.
