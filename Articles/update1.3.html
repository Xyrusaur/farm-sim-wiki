Farming Simulator 22 Update 1.3

Written By Xyrus Babao July 22, 2022
News

The team at Giants software have just released update 1.3! In the patch update they have included free vehicles: The Ploeger AT 5104 LNMS Self-Propelled Slurry Tanker, Massey Ferguson MF IDEAL 8PL Combine Harvester, Krampe Big Body 750 S Trailer, Fendt IDEAL 7PL Combine Harvester, Hardi Navigator 4000 Delta Force Sprayer and the Garant Kotte PQ 32,000 Slurry Tanker. I can't wait for this update to happen!

You can learn more at the original Farming Simulator Website.
