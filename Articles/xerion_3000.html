Claas Xerion 3000

Written By Xyrus Babao July 22, 2022
Mods / Vehicles / Large Tractors

The Claas Xerion 3000 is a high horsepower, large tractor that has a budgetable price of around $330,000. This tractor is great for users that have little budget and want to use it for high horsepower jobs. If you have added the trac vc feature to your xerion 3000, you can turn the cabin around to get a better view of attachments that can only go at the back of your tractor.

You can learn more at the original Modhub website.

Note: This mod has a bug that when you turn the cabin around, it should go the same speed as it goes forward but instead goes the normal reverse speed which is 26kph.

The specs:

Price: $330,200

Max speed: 50kph

Horsepower: 335 - 379hp

Pros:

  • A large high horsepower tractor with a cheap price.
  • Great for farmers who have spent some time to get a decent amount of money and need this tractor for heavy duty jobs or as a utility tractor.

Cons:

  • Is still a very expensive tractor because it is large.
