Rigitrac SKE 50

Written By Xyrus Babao July 22, 2022
Basegame / Vehicles / Small Tractors

The Rigitrac SKE 50 is small utility tractor for light jobs like vineyard work. It also uses an electric motor to save money instead of paying money for fuel. The problem about this tractor about it's electric motor is that the Rigitrac does not have enough horsepower to pull large tools like cultivators, trailers, etc.

You can learn more at the original Farming Simulator website.

The specs:

Price: $330,200

Max speed: 50kph

Horsepower: 335 - 379hp

Pros:

  • A small utility tractor that is great for vineyard work.
  • Great for farmers who need a tractor for small, quick jobs.

Cons:

  • Does not have the horsepower to tow most things.
